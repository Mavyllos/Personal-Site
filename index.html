About Me

A Brief Introduction.

Hi, my name is Summer!

I love to build a bridge between the products that developers create and the people who use those products.

My career in tech started in Arizona, as an analyst and then technical writer. I worked on a variety of projects—from graphic-heavy online course materials for e-learning to simple word-based technical documents and procedural materials. My focus was creating and maintaining API documentation for credit card processing software.

It was working with software development teams that led me to learn software development myself. In 2017, my husband and I jumped on an opportunity to move to Seattle, WA, where I enrolled in and graduated from Galvanize's web development immersive. Though the immersive taught the full-stack, I fell in love with front-end development.

I'm now looking for work as a technical writer, front-end developer, or a hybrid of the two.
